BLACK CORRELATION

Case study

Brief.

Black Correlation is an Australian luxury lifestyle brand. Their mission is to excel in quality across their digital footprint as an e-commerce site. As creative director I produced a fully responsive e-commerce website, created product campaigns and ran their social media.

Social media

The Black Correlation brand is known for its striking luxury image and minimal content. I created a visual narrative through creating photos and videos for the brand's social media to make sure the brand's image was represented correctly.

Responsive Website

I set out to build a fully responsive e-commerce website using the shopify platform. Through a clean, cool and clutter-free design, I captured the luxury minimal vibe of Black Correlation. I made sure the user visiting the site was always aware of all the great offers that they had to offer without feeling pressured to buy.
