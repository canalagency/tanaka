TRESS HAVEN.

As a new business moving into a trendy part of Brisbane, Tress Haven wanted to make a splash coming onto the scene. I was able to deliver the necessary ingredients to ensure their web presence matched the exquisite experience of stepping into the salon.

After advising Tress that they were missing out on tremendous customer base by not having a website set up and having no GOOGLE listing, I set out to develop a website, using a 4-step design process to discover client values and preferences. The resulting website was a simple and clean site that would serve as a digital brochure for its visitors. The site was then optimised for SEO and works across all browsers and device types such as mobiles, tablets, laptops, and desktops. 

When I first approached Tress, their Facebook and Instagram pages were not set up as business accounts and were missing a lot of vital information. I managed both their accounts and set them up properly making sure that potential customers would have no problem finding them. During the process of creating their social media pages, they began receiving an increase in inquiries on social media about their services.
