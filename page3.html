MIKOS BARBERSHOP

Brief:

I approached MIKOS BARBERSHOP to create a vision of the future and show what digital transformation could mean for their customers and their business.

The plan:

With a growing number of barbershops arriving in the Canberra area, it was apparent Mikos had to improve their digital marketing to stay on top of the market. I developed personas in order to understand customers needs and motivations, using them to create customer journeys through the business. In tandem, I defined a strategic vision to provide ideas and initiatives in response to what I'd learned about Mikos customers.

THE WORK

I showed MIKOS BARBERS how they could create a connected customer experience by responding to them in a timely, human way at every key life stage. I gave them instructions on how to run and manage their social media in order to give customers tools that create reasons to engage outside their normal behaviour..
